lightbulb Quick start
  1. Speak — type in the box, then press the blue speaker (or Enter).
  2. Buttons — in Everyday, tap the button to speak; the small + inserts text.
  3. Assign — after you have text, pin it to a new sound button.
  4. Drafts A–C — tap a letter to switch among three text drafts.
  5. Recent — tap to insert; double-tap to speak.
  6. Message — words under the box; tap × to remove one.
  7. Backup — switch to Builder, then Topics → Export / Import.
  8. Selection — highlight words in the box to speak only that part.
